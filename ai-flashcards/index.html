About SRS Scheduling

FlashAI uses a simplified SM-2 algorithm for spaced repetition.

Each card tracks an interval (days until next review) and an ease factor (default 2.5×).

• Again — resets the card; ease drops slightly.
• Hard — interval × 1.2; ease drops slightly.
• Good — interval × ease (the sweet spot).
• Easy — interval × ease × 1.3; ease increases.

Cards start at 1-day intervals and grow exponentially — showing them right before you'd forget them to maximise long-term retention.
